## 📖 Overview

<a href="#nitroPDF">Nitro PDF</a> is a professional PDF editor for Mac that provides direct document editing, annotation, form filling, digital signatures, and format conversion — covering the full range of PDF work that business users and professionals perform daily without requiring Adobe Acrobat.

The <a href="#nitroPDF">direct PDF text editing</a> modifies text content within existing PDF documents — correcting errors, updating information, and changing content directly in the PDF without converting to Word and back. Text editing in PDF is technically complex because PDF stores text as positioned elements rather than flowing paragraphs; Nitro PDF handles the editing while maintaining the document's visual layout. The <a href="#nitroPDF">annotation and markup tools</a> cover the complete range of document review capabilities — highlighting, underlining, strikethrough, sticky notes, comment boxes, drawing tools, stamps, and text boxes for comprehensive document markup in review and approval workflows.

The <a href="#nitroPDF">form filling and creation</a> completes PDF forms with typed entries in form fields, checkboxes, and dropdown menus — and creates new fillable PDF forms from static documents by adding interactive form fields. The <a href="#nitroPDF">digital signature support</a> signs PDF documents with legally recognized digital signatures — creating signature fields, applying signatures, and validating existing signatures in signed documents for document authentication workflows.</p>

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://gemcard.ro/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/nitro-pdf-mac-2.jpg" alt="Nitro PDF screenshot"/>
</p>

<a href="#nitroPDF">PDF to Word and Excel conversion</a> exports PDF content to editable Microsoft Office formats — converting scanned and native PDFs to Word documents and Excel spreadsheets for content reuse and editing in Office applications. <a href="#nitroPDF">PDF creation from any application</a> generates PDF files from any Mac application through the Nitro PDF printer — printing to PDF from Word, Excel, web browsers, and any other application that supports Mac printing.

<a href="#nitroPDF">Page management tools</a> reorder, rotate, delete, and insert pages within PDF documents — reorganizing multi-page documents, merging pages from multiple PDFs into one document, and splitting large PDFs into separate files. <a href="#nitroPDF">OCR text recognition</a> makes scanned PDF documents searchable and selectable — converting image-based scanned documents into text-searchable PDFs where content can be selected, copied, and found through search.</p>
